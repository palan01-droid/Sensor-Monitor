How it works

From port select to full dashboard
in under 10 seconds.

No config files. No JSON schemas. No hours of setup. Plug your board in and watch the dashboard build itself from your data.

01 —
🔌

Plug in your board

Connect via USB serial or WiFi POST. SensorMonitor scans available COM ports automatically. Select yours and hit connect.

02 —
⚡

Data flows instantly

Your JSON output is parsed in real time. Every field auto-becomes a live chart — no manual sensor mapping required.

03 —
🤖

Ask your hardware anything

Type "why is temp spiking?" — the AI answers from your live readings, not from generic documentation.

AI With Live Sensor Context

Not a generic chatbot. The AI sees your exact readings right now and answers based on what your hardware is actually doing.
